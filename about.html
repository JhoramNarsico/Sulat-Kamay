The Heart Behind the Handwriting

My journey wasn't a straight line; it was a climb. It began in the halls of Xavier University, graduating Cum Laude with a degree in Special Needs Education.


As a Licensed Professional Teacher, I learned that every person processes love differently. But when the classroom walls felt too small, I looked up.


Hiking became my escape. Scaling the mountains of Mindanao taught me that the best views come after the hardest climbs. It taught me patience, silence, and the beauty of slowing down.


Sulat Kamay is the summit of these passions. I bring the patience of a teacher and the peace of a hiker into every box I pack.
